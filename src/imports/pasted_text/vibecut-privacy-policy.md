# VibeCut Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 11, 2026

Welcome to **VibeCut**. Your privacy and the protection of your data are fundamental priorities for us. This Privacy Policy transparently and objectively describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

By using VibeCut, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

To provide VibeCut's core functionality (facial analysis and haircut suggestions), we collect specific types of data:

### 1.1. Data You Provide Directly

- **Facial Images:** We collect an image of your face strictly to perform the face shape analysis and suggest haircuts.
- **Important:** **We do not permanently store your facial image on our servers.** The image is instantly processed by our Artificial Intelligence and discarded immediately after the result is presented.

### 1.2. Automatically Collected Data

- **IP Address and Device Data:** We automatically collect technical information such as your IP address, device model, operating system version, and unique advertising identifiers (Advertising ID).
- **Usage Logs:** Crash logs and performance data to ensure App stability.

## 2. Device Permissions

For VibeCut to work correctly, we request the following permissions on your device:

- **Camera:** Required to capture a photo of your face in real time for the haircut analysis.
- **Gallery (Photos/Media):** Required if you choose to upload an existing photo from your device for analysis.

You may revoke these permissions at any time in your operating system settings, but doing so will prevent the App's main features from functioning.

## 3. Use of Artificial Intelligence and Biometrics

VibeCut uses Artificial Intelligence (AI) algorithms to analyze the geometry of your face.

- **Nature of the Data:** Facial geometry analysis is considered biometric data (sensitive personal data). We handle this data with the utmost care.
- **Purpose:** The analysis is performed exclusively to categorize face shape and recommend compatible haircut styles.
- **No Tracking:** We do not use this technology for personal identification, surveillance, or the creation of a persistent biometric database. The analysis is functional and ephemeral (temporary).

## 4. Advertising and Third Parties

VibeCut is offered free of charge and supported by advertising. For this purpose, we use third-party services:

- **Google AdMob:** We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google may use your device's advertising identifier and cookies to display personalized ads based on your interests.
- **Granular Consent (Opt-Out):** You are not required to receive personalized advertising to use the App. You may opt out of interest-based ads through your mobile device settings (Android/iOS) or directly through Google's ad settings.
- **Ad Age Rating:** We are committed to displaying only advertisements appropriate to the App's age rating. We configure our advertising partners to filter sensitive or inappropriate content, ensuring VibeCut is safe and complies with app store family content policies.
- To learn more about how Google handles your data, visit: [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads).

## 5. Purpose and Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data for the following purposes, supported by their respective legal bases under applicable privacy law:

- **Service Provision (Facial Analysis):** Processing your image to deliver the haircut suggestion. **Legal Basis: Consent** (processing of sensitive biometric data occurs with your authorization when you use the feature).
- **Improvement and Security:** Monitoring App usage to fix errors and prevent fraud. **Legal Basis: Legitimate Interest**.
- **Advertising:** Displaying relevant and safe advertisements through Google AdMob. **Legal Basis: Legitimate Interest or Consent** (depending on your device settings).

## 6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your photos or personal data to third parties. Sharing only occurs in the following situations:

- **Service Providers:** With technology partners (such as cloud services) strictly necessary for App operation.
- **Advertising Partners:** Technical and advertising identification data are shared with Google AdMob.
- **Legal Obligation:** If we are required to do so by competent judicial or governmental authorities.

## 7. Data Retention and Security

- **Images:** As mentioned, facial images are processed and deleted immediately after analysis. We do not maintain a database of user photos.
- **Logs and IP:** Log data may be retained for the minimum period required by law or as long as necessary for security and audit purposes.
- We adopt technical and administrative security measures (such as encryption in transit) to protect your data from unauthorized access.

## 8. Your Rights

As a data subject, applicable privacy law grants you the following rights, which may be exercised at any time:

- **Confirmation and Access:** The right to know whether we process your data and to access it.
- **Correction:** The right to request correction of incomplete, inaccurate, or outdated data.
- **Anonymization, Blocking, or Deletion:** The right to request deletion of unnecessary data or data processed in non-compliance with the law.
- **Right to Information:** The right to be informed about the possibility of not providing consent and the consequences of that decision (in this case, the inability to use the App's facial analysis feature).
- **Withdrawal of Consent:** The right to withdraw consent for data use, acknowledging that this may prevent use of the App.

To exercise your rights, please contact us at the email address provided at the end of this policy.

## 9. Children's Privacy

VibeCut is not intentionally directed at **children (persons under 13 years of age)**. We do not intentionally collect personal data from children. If we identify that we have collected data from a child without verified parental or guardian consent, we will take steps to delete that information as quickly as possible.

## 10. International Data Transfers

Some of our partners (such as Google and AI processing servers) may be located outside of Brazil. In such cases, we ensure that transfers occur to countries with an adequate level of data protection or through the use of standard contractual clauses that guarantee information security.
